About Us

This project, TRANQUIL-TALK, is a platform designed to facilitate healing and support through confidential conversations. It was inspired by personal experiences of overcoming challenges with the help of supportive communities.

Developed as a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, TRANQUIL-TALK encourages users to explore their emotions in a safe environment and seek guidance from professionals or peers.
